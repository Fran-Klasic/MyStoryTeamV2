Overview

My Story Team is an application that allows users to create planners for their short-term, mid-term, and long-term goals. By using the app, users can organize their plans more effectively, track their progress, and stay motivated throughout the entire process. The application offers a clear and simple way to manage tasks and goals, making it easier and faster to turn plans into reality.

Our Story

People often have big life goals such as education, career, health, or personal development, but they frequently face the same challenges: they do not know where to start, they see their goals as too distant or unattainable, they lose motivation and give up, and they neglect basic habits such as sleep, health, and daily routines.

This represents a significant social issue. When people fail to reach their full potential, levels of stress, anxiety, and burnout increase. More and more young people give up on education, work, or personal ambitions, and society gradually loses productive, healthy, and motivated individuals.

The planner is based on a circle system:

  1. The smallest circle represents daily plans
  2. The medium circle represents short-term goals
  3. The largest circle represents long-term goals spanning several years
